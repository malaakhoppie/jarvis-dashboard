## Core Problem

Mala has a real edge and high R:R setups. The strategy is not the issue.

The problem is discomfort tolerance. The moment he's down ~$500, ego takes over — limits get removed, revenge trades happen, and the account blows.

---

## Behavioral Patterns

- **Self-sabotage at the threshold of success** — gets funded, makes money, then panics and nukes everything within 1-2 days
- **Addicted to "I can make it back right now"** — chases the rush instead of respecting rules
- **Cannot sit with a small loss** — would rather blow the account than accept a red day
- **Avoidance via new goals** — adds more businesses, plans, and projects to avoid facing the one real problem: closing the laptop when the daily loss limit hits
- **Hype-then-collapse cycle** — talks big, gets motivated, makes detailed plans, then misses the uncomfortable moment and goes full revenge mode
- **Post-blowup spiral** — self-pity → days off → fresh hype → repeat
